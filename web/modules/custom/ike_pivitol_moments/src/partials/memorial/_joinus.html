Make a Contribution

Join us in honoring and celebrating Ike’s life and legacy by memorializing him in our Nation’s capital.

The memorial is projected for completion in 2019, the 75th anniversary of D-Day. The American public is rallying together to help the Commission achieve a fundraising goal of $35 million from the private sector. We have a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to make this vital memorial a reality. Today, we invite you to join us in recognizing a true American hero. Thank you for standing up and saying, “I Like Ike!”

Donate online at pay.gov

or

Checks payable to Dwight D. Eisenhower Memorial Commission can be mailed to:

1629 K Street, N. W. Suite 801
Washington, D.C. 20006
Attn: Joyce Jacobson

Donations are considered as gifts to the United States and are tax deductible under Section 170(c) of the United States Internal Revenue Code. Your donation will go directly into a separate Treasury Account specifically for the construction of the memorial

For additional information or questions, contact Joyce Jacobson: jjacobson@eisenhowermemorial.gov
